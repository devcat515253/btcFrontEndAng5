General

What is account in Smartex Exchange and why do I need it?

Account in Smartex Exchange is your virtual offcie where you can see everything aboutyour activity on Smartex.info. You need to have an account to connect your payments with your person according to relevant laws.

How can I check order status?

You can easily check status of the order here. In order to check order status you need to enter email address (which was used for the order), order ID and Captcha.

Can I cancel my order?

If the order is not completed, you can cancel it by contacting support. Please provide the reason you wish to cancel the order. Once the order is completed and payment is sent, it is not possible to cancel the order and refund it.
